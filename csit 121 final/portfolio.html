Featured project #1: "My favorite recipe"
So, at the start of the semester, we kicked off this cool project named "My Favorite Recipe." Basically, the deal was to build up a simple website using HTML and CSS, all about our ultimate go-to recipe and why we liked it. Over the course of the semester, our website became more and more interactive. We picked up loads of new tricks and topics that we incorporated into our website as we went along. For example, we inclued a nutritional table, forms and even multimedia! It's been a wild ride of learning and tinkering, turning my site into this awesome mix of tech skills and culinary passion.
