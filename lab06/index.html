Ingredients and Instructions:

Ingredients:

  • 3-5 pounds of Chicken (Breast, Thighs, Drumsticks)
  • 1/2 cup of jerk seasoning
  • About 1/4 of soy sauce
  • 4-6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash and dry the chicken.
  2. Prepare the marinade: Blend jerk seasoning, soy sauce, minced garlic, vegetable oil, brown sugar, ground cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  3. Coat the chicken with the marinade and chill for 4+ hours or overnight.
  4. Preheat the grill to 350-400째F.
  5. Grill the chicken for 20-30 minutes, turning until the internal temperature reaches 165째F.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
