Jerk chicken

Recipe created by Charles

Ingredients:

  • 3-5 pounds of Chicken (Breast, Thighs, Drumsticks)
  • 1/2 cup of jerk seasoning
  • About 1/4 of soy sauce
  • 4-6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 4-6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash and Dry chicken
  2. Make Marinade: Blend seasoning, oil, soy sauce, sugar, garlic, peppers, onion, spices, salt, and pepper.
  3. Coat chicken with marinade and chill 4+ hours or overnight.
  4. Preheat grill to 350-400°F
  5. Grill chicken 20-30 mins, turning until internal temp is 165°F
  6. Serve and enjoy

This is my favorite recipe because, I grew up eating this whenever my grandma made it for me and it makes me feel nostalgic.
